## Connection

The track was catalysed by an atelier workshop for a sovereign growth-stage investor client (`atelier/workspaces/sggc-ai-transformation-workshop/`). Workshop design surfaced that sovereign growth-stage investors, fund-of-funds, family offices, endowments, and pension pools represent a sizable underserved segment for autonomy-first portfolio management — but Astra's current engine is public-markets-native and does not apply.

The shared primitives (decision-brief, debate, graduated autonomy L0–L4, automatic demotion, forensic audit) transfer cleanly. The engine primitives do not. New primitives are needed (deal-flow triage, DD orchestration, KPI monitoring, Level-3 mark-to-model, cap-table evolution, stage-transition gates, thesis-drift detection, exit-pathway reasoning). See the authoritative spec for full specification.
